## Finding organisms

Click on "Organisms" in the top navigation bar to access the full list of species available on [BRC-analytics.org](https://BRC-analytics.org).

<Figure
  src="/learn/getting-started/what-are-organisms/organisms-nav.jpeg"
  alt="Click on Organisms in the navigation"
/>

## Browsing and filtering

The organisms page displays all available species. Use the filter panel on the left to narrow the list by taxonomy, genome availability, or other criteria.

<Figure
  src="/learn/getting-started/what-are-organisms/organisms-list.jpeg"
  alt="List of organisms with filter panel"
/>

## Using the starburst widget

You can also select any taxon directly from the home page using the interactive starburst widget.

<Figure
  src="/learn/getting-started/what-are-organisms/starburst-widget.jpeg"
  alt="Starburst widget for selecting taxa"
/>
